# SignalLab Demo Script (2 Minutes)

## 1. The Problem (30s)
*   **Scene**: Face camera or "Problem" slide.
*   **Script**: "Scientific research is broken. Data is siloed, provenance is lost, and funding is opaque. We can't verify where data comes from, and researchers struggle to monetize their work without giving up ownership. The current system relies on trust, but trust is failing."

## 2. The Solution (30s)
*   **Scene**: Show "Solution" slide or SignalLab landing page.
*   **Script**: "Enter SignalLab. A decentralized research registry built on Solana. We provide cryptographic provenance for datasets, on-chain governance for funding, and direct monetization for researchers. By anchoring data hashes to the blockchain, we ensure integrity while keeping storage costs low."

## 3. Live Demo (30s)
*   **Scene**: Screen share of the app.
*   **Action**: Connect Wallet -> Create Project -> Add Dataset.
*   **Script**: "Let's see it live. I connect my Phantom wallet. I create a new project called 'Quantum Bio-Data'. It's on-chain instantly. Now, I upload a dataset. The app hashes it locally—SHA-256—and only the fingerprint goes on-chain. This 'Bio-Enrichment' is now immutable and verifiable by anyone, anywhere."

## 4. Why Solana? (30s)
*   **Scene**: Show the 'Signal Stream' feed.
*   **Script**: "Why Solana? Speed and cost. We need high-frequency updates for our 'Signal Stream'—a real-time feed of scientific activity. With Solana, we pay fractions of a cent per proof, enabling granular tracking that's impossible on other chains. SignalLab isn't just a registry; it's the nervous system of open science."
